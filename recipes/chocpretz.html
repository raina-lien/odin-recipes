Chocolate Pretzels

Description

The perfect movie nights snack, late-night bite, or just to munch on while dinner's cooking... I won't tell you how to enjoy these delicious and oh-so-easy chocolate pretzels! Made with just two basic ingredients, these treats taste like a million bucks.

Ingredients

  • A handful of pretzels (doesn't matter what kind or shape)
  • A small bar of chocolate (again, pick your favorite flavor!)
  • Optional: Sea salt, chili, sprinkles, another type of chocolate, any add-ons that you're craving

Steps

  1. Melt chocolate.
  2. Dip pretzels in chocolate and lay on waxed paper.
  3. Sprinkle or drizzle any add-ins on top.
  4. Let chocolate harden at room temperature or in the fridge.

Enjoy!
