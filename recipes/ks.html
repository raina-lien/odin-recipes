Korv Stroganoff (Swedish Sausage Stroganoff)

Description

Korvstroganoff, or Swedish sausage stroganoff, is a delicious, easy meal that is perfect for hurried weeknights and picky eaters. It's also relatively inexpensive and easy to throw together with pantry staples. If you're in Sweden, falukorv is the obvious choice, but for those in other countries, hot dogs or un-sliced bologna (or any mild sausage) works fabulously as well. Serve with rice, pasta, or potatoes (or even roasted cauliflower for a low-carb twist), parsley on top, and sliced raw veggies on the side!

Ingredients (Serves 4)

  • 400 g falukorv or other sausage
  • 0.5 cup creme fraiche
  • 0.5 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 0.25 cup tomato paste
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 1 tsp beef stock concentrate
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked rice, pasta, potatoes, or any other grain/carb to serve the stroganoff atop
  • Raw veggies to eat on the side (cucumber and tomatoes are my favorite!)
